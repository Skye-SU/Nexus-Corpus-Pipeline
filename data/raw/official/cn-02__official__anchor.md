## Notes

English-language version of the Beijing Internet Court's first-instance judgment on AI-generated image copyright.

## Working Abstract

This judgment by the Beijing Internet Court addresses whether an image generated using Stable Diffusion qualifies as a copyrightable work and whether the user who created it can be recognized as the author. The court held that the plaintiff's selection of models, parameters, and prompts constituted sufficient intellectual contribution to support authorship and copyright protection under Chinese law.
