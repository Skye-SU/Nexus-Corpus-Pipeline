## Notes

Future of Music Coalition Bluesky post on AI-material disclosure requirements at copyright registration.

## Working Abstract

The post notes that the Copyright Office requires disclosure of AI-generated material at the time of registration but does not require disclosure of analytic AI tools such as mastering plug-ins. It links to the relevant Federal Register document.
